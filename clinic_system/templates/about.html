Our Story

Dermacare Clinic was founded with one simple mission: to make quality healthcare accessible, transparent, and genuinely personal. We bring together experienced specialists, modern facilities, and a digital platform that lets you book, consult, and manage your health from anywhere.

From dermatology and general medicine to pediatrics and cosmetic care, every patient receives the same standard of attention — unhurried, evidence-based, and tailored to their needs.

Our Mission

To raise the standard of medical care in our community by combining clinical excellence with a warm, considered patient experience.

  • Excellence — board-certified specialists across all departments.
  • Innovation — digital records, online booking, in-house pharmacy.
