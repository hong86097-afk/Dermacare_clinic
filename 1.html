About Dermacare Clinic

Dermacare Clinic is a modern healthcare center in Phnom Penh dedicated to providing personalized dermatology and general medical services. Our mission is to combine compassionate care with advanced technology to ensure every patient receives precise treatment and a seamless experience.

From the moment you book an appointment to the time you receive your prescription, our integrated system keeps everything simple, efficient, and patient-focused.

Our Laboratory

Our in-house laboratory is equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic tools to support doctors in delivering evidence-based care. Patients benefit from fast, accurate results and smooth communication between clinicians and lab technicians.

  • ✔ Routine blood and urine analysis
  • ✔ Skin biopsy and pathology services
  • ✔ Allergy and sensitivity testing
  • ✔ Preventive health screenings

By integrating laboratory services directly into our clinic platform, we reduce waiting times and improve treatment outcomes.
